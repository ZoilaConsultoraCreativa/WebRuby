# **App Name**: LeadVoice

## Core Features:

- Program Showcase: Dedicated pages for each of the three core programs: individual coaching, intensive group program, and subscription service, detailing their benefits and structure.
- Company Success Stories: Highlight Ruby's impact on businesses with compelling case studies, demonstrating her experience and the value she brings to corporate clients.
- AI-Powered Client Persona Tool: Generative AI tool that intakes user responses and creates a preliminary 'Ideal Client Profile' and report based on those details.
- Style Aligned Testimonials: Client testimonials section in tone/style and palette with visual assets to show the personality/benefits alignment with brand. 
- Direct Engagement Forms: Clear forms for potential clients to quickly inquire about specific programs or corporate services, streamlining the initial contact process.
- Social Proof Highlights: Use a generative AI tool that highlights client reviews and converts them to social posts. Use a tool that incorporates aspects of Ruby's style and integrates the correct hex colors into a branded background
- Personality Integration Tool: Integrate a generative AI tool trained to respond to client inquiries in the persona of Ruby Villarroel

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Terracota (#C2573A) for a warm, approachable, and confident feel, inspired by the brand's emphasis on human connection.
- Background color: Blush Beige Claro (#FCF1E4) for a clean and elegant base, enhancing the content's readability and creating a sophisticated atmosphere.
- Accent color: Verde Salvia (#BCC9AD) to convey wellness, balance, and a gentle, trustworthy touch.
- Headline font: 'Playfair', a serif, for headlines to evoke elegance, trust and intellectualism. Body font: 'PT Sans', a sans-serif, is modern and works perfectly to display longer content.
- Use simple and modern icons that resonate with leadership, communication, and personal growth.
- Employ a clean and modern layout, emphasizing readability and easy navigation.
- Use subtle animations to enhance user experience, such as smooth transitions and interactive elements upon user actions.
